Dogs in States.

The above data represents the relation between total number of households within the state and number of dog owning households.
The vertical height of the bars is directly proportional to the amount of the measure. Higher bars meaning higher quantity.
Questions I can ask myself before starting a business in my state related to:
  1. Dog food and accessories.

  2. Dog's day care center.

  3. Supply of Dog's medicines.

  4. Dog's breeding center.

  5. As dog owners are mostly extroverts so I want to settle in such states or not?
Conclusions I can derive:
  1. In states like California and New York 1/3rd of total households have dogs. Meaning every 1 out of 3 houses have a dog. Thus states with higher population of dog owners like California and New York are good for selling large amount of products.

  2. Whereas in Mississippi and Idaho 1/2 of the total households have dogs. Meaning every second house has a dog, but being less populated, they will not serve well for large businesses.

  3. Many dogs within state means many dog parks.

  4. Many dogs within state means many dog breeding centers to buy a dog from.


Cats in States.

The above data represents the relation between total number of households within the states and number of cat owning households.
The vertical height of the bars is directly proportional to the amount of the measure. Higher bars meaning higher quantity.
Questions I can ask myself before starting a business in a state related to:
  1. Cat's food and accessories.

  2. Supply of Cat's medicines.

  3. Cat's breeding center.

  4. As cat owners are mostly introverts, so do I want to settle in such states or not?
Conclusions I can derive:
  1. In states like California, New York and Texas 1/4th of total households have cats. Meaning every 1 out of 4 houses have a cat. However, since these states have a higher population so they can become the hotspots for my business.

  2. Whereas in Washington and Florida 1/3rd of the total households have cats. Meaning every 1 out of 3 houses has a cat. However, since they are less populated, they might not serve well to start a big business.

  3. Thus according to the size of my business I might see which state might serve me well. I would choose a state with larger number of cat owners and also a larger population as well.

  4. Many cats within state means many cat breeding centers to buy cats from.
